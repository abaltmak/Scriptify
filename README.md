# Scriptify
For: Students. <br> <br>
Who: have trouble with writing long texts by hand, or just get bored quickly.<br> <br>
Have you ever waited until Sunday, to write a long essay for Monday?<br> <br>
The solution is unlike anything you have ever seen:  
<h1> <ins>Scriptify</ins> </h1> </br>
<ol>
  <li>Scriptify is an app where you write your essays digitally, add a background and print.</li>
  <li>Scriptify will ask you to upload photos of your handwriting, by writing the alphabet 3 times.</li>
  <li>Our app will randomize the letters, to make any text look like you wrote it on paper by pen.</li>
  <li>Scriptify will correct you mistakes, and make it look like you corrected them, giving you authenticity and great marks.</li>
  <li>In our app we have custom settings, where you can choose what level of language you want our app to use in it’s corrections.</li>
  <li>All settings can be altered by the user.</li>
  <li>You can write up to 300 words per week for no cost, however, if you choose to buy our silver subscription for 3.99$ per month, and get unlimited amount of words.</li>
  <li>For our gold subscription our app will also draw anything you need in maths, and even solve high maths problems.</li>
  <li>Scriptify will explain to you why it corrected certain mistakes, or for our gold customers, it will act as a digital teacher, explaining mathematical problems like a ivy league professor.</li>
</ol>
